title: "Regulating AI in Education Policy: A Comparative Study of EU approaches"
---

What happens when five EU countries face the same regulatory framework but respond in completely different ways? This presentation takes you inside the governance of AI in education — a high-risk domain under the EU AI Act. We will walk through a comparative analysis of five EU member states, examine how each defines the role of the state in AI education policy, and reveal the distinct governance models that emerge. Along the way, we challenge the assumption that EU membership produces regulatory convergence, and make the case for why sector-specific analysis is not just useful, but rather necessary.
