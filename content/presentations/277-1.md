title: "“Augmented Pragmatism” – Applying Aristotelian Virtue Ethics to Discussions Surrounding AI"
---

Applying Aristotelian virtue ethics to discussions on AI is an under-
represented but growing trend. I analyse the debate surrounding Autonomous
Vehicles to argue that virtue ethics is the only philosophical framework through
which current discussions on AI ethics can be satisfactorily modelled. I propose
to view virtue ethics as an “Augmented Pragmatism” – whereas pragmatism,
operating on only one variable of efficiency or profit-maximisation, is in itself
too narrow to accommodate for the complexity of AI and its societal implica-
tions, virtue ethics admits more variables: explainability, social responsibility,
sustainability, etc. Lastly, I argue that this framework is consistent with the
practices of engineers.
