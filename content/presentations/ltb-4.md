title: "Computer Vision for Automated Car Part Identification"
---

"Winner of the Microsoft Imagine Cup Junior AI for Good category, this project is a system for
sorting used car parts by recyclability and value. We combine Azure Custom Vision object
detection with a calibrated, 3D-printed robot arm to identify parts from a live video feed and
perform sorting. I will describe the overview of the system structure employed, outline how the
training and testing datasets were obtained, and explain how model predictions were filtered and
processed. After expanding on some further software and hardware intricacies in this project, I
will conclude by evaluating the system’s performance and recommending improvements."
