title: "Exploring the Application of LLM-Simulated Oxford Tutorials for Critical Thinking and Assessment In the Humanities"
---

"Oxford-style tutorials train articulation, objection-handling, and critical thinking
under pressure, crucial skills in the humanities. Yet they are difficult to scale because they
depend on intensive one-to-one teaching. This paper argues that LLMs can be developed into
scalable tools that can function as a dialogical ‘Oxford’ tutor to overcome common concerns
about LLM use in education. I emphasise two requirements: preserve the tutorial structure to
promote critical thinking and avoid homogeneity and combat hallucination via citation
requirements for verification. Based on a sample LLM transcript, I conclude that there is
good reason to be optimistic about this use case in humanities education."
