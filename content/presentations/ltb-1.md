title: "FastEcho: One-step Controllable Echocardiogram Video Synthesis"
---

FastEcho is a one-step generative framework for controllable echocardiogram video synthesis. We extend latent flow matching to generate full cardiac videos while conditioning on clinically meaningful parameters, demonstrated through precise control of left-ventricular ejection fraction (EF). Unlike diffusion-based approaches that require many iterative denoising steps, FastEcho enables temporally coherent video generation in a single inference step. The model generates 18.5 videos per second, representing a 50× speed-up over multi-step linear flow baselines while maintaining competitive or superior quality metrics. In clinician evaluation, experts were close to chance when distinguishing real from synthetic videos, indicating strong perceptual realism.
