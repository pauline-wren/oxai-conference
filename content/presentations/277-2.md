title: "Monetisation and Product Growth in the Age of LLMs: Selling Through Conversation"
---

Large Language Models are redefining how products are discovered, recommended, and purchased. This talk explores how conversational AI dismantles traditional growth funnels, replacing static experiences with dynamic, context-aware interactions embedded directly in chat. Drawing on real-world product patterns — conversational commerce, AI-assisted upselling, and personalised recommendations — the session examines what monetisation looks like when the interface is a conversation. It also addresses the trust and UX considerations that make or break these models. Attendees will leave with a practical, product-first lens on how LLMs are reshaping digital business — and what to build next.
